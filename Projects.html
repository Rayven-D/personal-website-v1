Cell Simulation

The cell simulation is a program that simulates a colony of cells growth and deaths. it will be comparing each cell with their neighbors, and determining if the cell will die or be alive in the next step, doing this to every cell for 100 steps. This program makes use of multiprocessing to handle the arrays, and it was tested with 10,000 x 10,000 array size, only taking less than 20 minutes with 36 processes.
